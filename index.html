What is DriveMitra?

DriveMitra is a cutting-edge Level 1 Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) designed to enhance vehicle safety and driver confidence through intelligent technology.

Object Detection

Real-time identification of vehicles, pedestrians, and obstacles with 99.7% accuracy.
